# BookLeaf Publishing — Author Knowledge Base

## Publishing Timelines
After final manuscript submission, the standard publishing timeline is 45 to 60 days.
This includes editorial review (7-10 days), formatting and layout (10-15 days),
cover design approval (5-7 days), ISBN registration (3-5 days), and
distribution setup across platforms (7-10 days).
Expedited publishing is available for authors with the Premium Package — timeline reduces to 25-30 days.

## Book Live Status
A book is considered "live" when it appears on Amazon, Flipkart, and the BookLeaf store.
Authors receive an email notification with the live links on the day of launch.
If the book_live_date has passed but the book is not visible, authors should check their
registered email for the launch notification, then contact support with their ISBN.

## Royalty Reports and Payments
Royalties are calculated on a quarterly basis: January, April, July, and October.
Payments are processed within 60 days of the quarter close.
Royalty status values: pending (not yet calculated), processing (payment initiated),
paid (funds transferred), on_hold (bank details issue or disputed sales).
Authors can view detailed royalty breakdowns in their dashboard under "Earnings."

## Dashboard Access
The author dashboard is at dashboard.bookleafpub.com.
Login uses the email address provided at registration.
If an author cannot log in, they should use "Forgot Password" or email support@bookleafpub.in.
The dashboard shows book status, sales numbers, royalty history, and add-on service status.

## Add-On Services
Bestseller Package: Targets Amazon and Flipkart bestseller categories through keyword optimization
and coordinated launch-day review campaigns. Active for 90 days from book live date.
PR Push: Press release distribution to 50+ media outlets and book blogs. Delivered within
30 days of book going live.
Award Submission: Manuscript submission to 3 curated literary awards relevant to the book genre.
Confirmation of submission provided within 45 days of book going live.

## Author Copies
Physical author copies are dispatched within 7-10 business days of the book going live.
Tracking details are sent to the registered email. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days.
International shipping takes 15-21 business days.
If the author copy has not arrived within 30 days of dispatch, authors should contact support.

## Sales Reports
Sales data is updated every 48 hours on the dashboard.
Monthly consolidated reports are emailed on the 5th of every month.
Sales figures include all platforms: Amazon India, Amazon Global, Flipkart, BookLeaf Store.

## Contacting Support
Email: support@bookleafpub.in
Response time: within 24 business hours.
For urgent issues, mention "URGENT" in the subject line.
