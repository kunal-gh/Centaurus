# Centaurus Operations Manual

## Publishing Timelines
After final manuscript submission, the standard publishing timeline is 45 to 60 days.
This usually includes editorial review, interior formatting, cover approval,
metadata setup, ISBN registration, and distribution preparation.
Expedited tracks can reduce the end-to-end timeline when the production queue and assets are ready.

## Release Status
A book is considered "live" when it appears on the primary retail and distribution channels assigned to the release.
Authors usually receive a launch confirmation email with the first live links on release day.
If the planned live date has passed but the title is not visible, operations should verify metadata sync,
retailer propagation delay, and any unresolved checklist items.

## Royalty Reports And Payments
Royalties are calculated quarterly: January, April, July, and October.
Payments are typically processed within 60 days of quarter close.
Status values include pending, processing, paid, and on_hold.
If a royalty is on hold, the most common causes are incomplete bank details, a compliance review, or a disputed adjustment.

## Workspace Access
The author workspace uses the onboarding link sent during setup and the registered email address.
If a user cannot sign in, they should first reset their password and then confirm they are using the same email tied to their profile.
If access still fails, route the case to ops@centaurus.dev with the user email and book title.

## Service Programs
Launch Sprint: category positioning, launch coordination, and short-run marketplace visibility support for the release window.
Media Relay: press outreach, publicity asset packaging, and external communications support after launch.
Awards Circuit: submission coordination for selected literary prizes and recognition programs based on genre fit and eligibility windows.

## Author Copies
Physical author copies are typically dispatched within 7 to 10 business days of a book going live.
Tracking details are usually sent to the registered email after shipment is booked.
If a copy has not arrived within 30 days of dispatch, operations should verify the shipping address and courier handoff.

## Sales Reports
Sales data is refreshed on a rolling basis and may lag behind marketplace events.
Monthly consolidated reporting is normally sent during the first week of the month.
When discussing sales with authors, make it clear whether the number is near-real-time platform data or a settled reporting figure.

## Escalation Policy
If identity is unclear, if a payment question involves risk, or if the available evidence is weak, the request should be routed to human review.
Centaurus should prefer safe escalation over speculative answers.
